What is Atmosfair

Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) in association with Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC) Start-Up, Bharati Vidyapeeth’s College of Engineering (Deemed to be University) College of Engineering, Pune is coming up with a blissful 2-day event “ATMOSFAIR”. This event is open for all students of all semesters & prestigious branches of BV(DU)COEP, as keen participants, enthusiastic performers, visionary entrepreneurs, or curious audiences. This is an enormous peer-to-peer opportunity with a vision for students to showcase their skills and fortes, and express their thoughtful ideas through various brilliant events that follow. This is one of a kind, rare opportunity for all the participants to learn from each other and add to their development and upskilling.

What Atmosfair offers

  • An experiential initiative as a form of brand activation makes it incredibly appealing for all the attendees and allows a rich mix of content, and inspirations from a variety of perspectives.
  • Participation from diverse departments of BV(DU)COEP where they will be exhibiting their extraordinary ideas on the well-planned mini-events before the student audience.
  • The astounding session hosted by a well-experienced professional mainly focused on building the blocks of entrepreneurship among the audience.
  • Enlightening ideas for Start-Ups that will be pitched by budding entrepreneurs before renowned investors, Mr. Indranil Chitale, Namita Thapar (Tentative), and many more.
  • This event will also outline flash mobs, Stand-ups by eminent Comedians, giving it more unifying priority than any other standalone event.
